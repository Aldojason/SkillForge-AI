# 🚀 SkillForge AI

> Your AI-powered placement preparation platform.

SkillForge AI is a full-stack SaaS web application designed to help students prepare for software engineering placements through intelligent study planning, DSA tracking, AI resume analysis, interview preparation, and performance analytics.

---

## ✨ Vision

To provide students with a single platform where they can plan, practice, track, and improve every aspect of placement preparation.
