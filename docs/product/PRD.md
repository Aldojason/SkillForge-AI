# Vision

SkillForge AI helps students prepare for software engineering placements by combining DSA tracking, study planning, AI-powered resume analysis, interview preparation, and analytics into one platform.

Instead of using multiple websites like LeetCode, Notion, Resume Worded, and Google Calendar separately, students can manage everything from one dashboard.

---

# Problem Statement

Students preparing for placements struggle because their preparation is scattered across many different platforms.

Typical workflow:

- Solve DSA on LeetCode
- Track progress in Excel
- Make study plans in Notion
- Build resumes separately
- Prepare interviews manually

This creates unnecessary complexity.

---

# Solution

SkillForge AI centralizes the entire placement preparation journey.

Users can:

- Track DSA
- Plan studies
- Build resumes
- Get AI resume feedback
- Practice interviews
- Track progress
- Purchase Premium features
